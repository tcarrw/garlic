jammy garlic toast · 10–12 min

A friendly baseline for any kitchen. Adjust heat & salt to taste.

Gold toast ≈ 55%

Ingredients

  • 2 thick bread slices
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, lightly smashed
  • 2–3 tbsp olive oil (or butter)
  • Salt + pepper (optional chilli)
  • Optional: parsley, lemon zest, parmesan

Steps

  1. Pan on medium-low. Add oil + smashed garlic.
  2. Stir 90–120s until garlic turns jammy (soft, fragrant; not browned). Remove garlic to a plate.
  3. Add bread to the garlicky oil; toast side A to golden.
  4. Flip; toast side B. If dry, add a little oil.
  5. Salt lightly; smear the soft garlic on top.
  6. Finish: pepper, parsley/lemon/parmesan if you like. Eat while warm.

Safety: if garlic browns early, lower heat or remove garlic and restart oil.

optional · clear voice (60–90s)

A tiny, kitchen-safe unwind you can do after plating.

“clear air, clear voice.”
  1. Breath 4–6 ×3: inhale 4s, exhale 6s.
  2. Hum 20s (mmm), jaw loose, lips together.
  3. Tongue drop 20s: tongue rests low, tip behind lower teeth.
  4. One yawn-sigh and sip water. Done.
