üë• Two-Player Adjustments

1. Trading

  • No direct trades between players.
  • You may trade only with the bank at 4:1, or use ports (3:1 or 2:1) if available.

2. Neutral Settlements (Optional)

  • After setup, each player places one neutral settlement and two neutral roads.
  • Neutral pieces block space but do not collect resources or score points.

3. The Robber

  • When moving the robber, you must steal from your opponent if possible.
  • If your opponent has no resource cards, you skip stealing.

4. Development Cards

  • Use the full deck as normal.
  • Victory Point cards remain secret until they cause a win.
