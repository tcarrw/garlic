Garlic: bulb of courage, root of warmth.

It hums beneath the knife, sharp then sweet.
A single clove holds memory of soil and fire —
a whisper that wakes the pan, the body, the day.

Roasted, raw, crushed, or whole — it never lies.
It simply arrives and fills the air
with truth strong enough to make you tear up a little.
